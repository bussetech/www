The studio portal is live at the apex domain. It is a renderer over the
studio's machine-readable state: projects come from the platform registry,
gnomes from the gnome registry, and the "latest" stream from each project
site's `feed.json`. Nothing on it is maintained by hand.

Posts like this one are the studio's own news feed — published at
[`/feed.json`](/feed.json) and [`/feed.xml`](/feed.xml), the same convention
every project site follows.

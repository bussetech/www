# This cycle in the studio

**Pulse:** a short, productive cycle — the lights are on and the next build chapter is opening.

The studio began scoping the next phase of the GenMURK text-world rebuild, working through the design questions that decide how the world resets, how players earn and keep progress, and how the build researches the real places it models — each recorded openly with a recommendation and a decision deadline. Alongside that, a routine check of the live sites caught a measurement gap: several of the studio's public sites weren't emitting their visitor-analytics beacon, so we weren't collecting traffic data from them. The cause is understood and a fix is queued, with a follow-up to make sure a site can never again look configured while quietly collecting nothing. Routine standup and cost reporting continued on schedule.

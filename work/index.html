How a study earns its place here

Case studies move through gates — consent, scrub, client review, operator merge — and a stalled gate simply doesn't publish. Client work appears only by recorded consent, scrubbed, under its codename. The studio's flagship study is about the studio itself, because the honest first client for this machinery was us.

An honest negative is data too.

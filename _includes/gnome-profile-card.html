{%- comment -%} Gnome profile card (portal-scoped, www#gnome-portraits). A "team page" treatment of the theme's gnome-card: same information, plus the gnome's generated portrait for its earned craft-ladder stage. Portraits are vendored under assets/gnomes//illus1..5.png (one per stage: novice=1 … meister=5; platform igotchi/illustrations.md §2). A gnome is drawn with a portrait only when it appears in _data/gnome_portraits.yml; otherwise it falls back to the igotchi text glyph, so the card never breaks on a not-yet-generated cast member. Per platform illustrations.md §3 the signature accent lives ONLY in the image plane — it never colours card chrome here. The portrait supplies all colour; the card stays neutral theme ink/gray, like the igotchi ruling.
